Gallery - Rendered Scenes

Could it be..?

A rendered painting bust of Ame Kumo's butterfly dragon character! I really love the colours and the design of this closed species. I tried something new with the shading here and it was much faster than my normal method of shading. I'm trying to zoom out more when drawing so that 'less is more', so to speak.

This art is Ame's prize for winning my raffle on EA!

Moondrop

A quick painting I made of Cassandra's moon drop transformation from the Tangled TV series! She looks so cool, but I'm sad that she was tricked into betraying Rapunzel. I hope she is redeemed in the end. I referenced the screenshot from the episode trailer!

Cr0wn

This is a stylized art of my OC, Cr0wn, otherwise known as Sam. I really like the style I used for this, and I hope I can replicate it in the future and that this isn't purely a fluke.

Would you like to dance?

For a content on NA, I drew a gijinka version of my northling OC, Sonder! I enjoy otome games, so I wanted to draw him as a romancable character in one. A fake screenshot, if you will. It would be cool if I could make my own otome game one day, but my anime drawing skills definitely need some work before then.

Would you dance with him? I'm surprising he had the courage to ask, since Sonder is normally a very shy and withdrawn persona.

Insomnia

One of my favourite commissions I did for Inktober 2019. This was a commissions for Eeveeandocs on Instagram! I relate to this character. Spending long nights up in front of a screen, staring at broken code, wanting desperately to sleep but knowing you have a project that is due the next day...

Wonder

I love the style I managed in this drawing! I tried to utilize a field blur affect and it really made things look good. I also changed my painting style a smidge- I added eyelashes and ribs on the horn, the eyes are different and I spent less time on the hair. I like the soft, elegant look it turned out with though! I hope I can draw something this good again.

This art was a gift for my friend, Platinum Feather on EA! She has a lovely OC and I can't wait to try redrawing this next year to see how much I've improved in my art!

Voidless

Never drew a twi'lek before (I think that is what the species is called?). I've seen them on Star Wars though and they are really cool. This is one of my first tries digitally painting a humanoid character! I really want to get good at painting humans one day, and I think this is a decent start.

This art is part of an art trade with my friend, CaraSilvaArt on DeviantArt! She has awesome art, so you should check her out!
