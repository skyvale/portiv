Gallery - Rendered Scenes

Believe

A very peaceful drawing. I wish I could sit in a tree and watch the fireflies. I don't think they have them where I live now- plus, there are much more dangerous bugs and parasites around. Nope.

Honeydew

This art is the result of an art swap with my best friend, Mossillus on Instagram! I did the sketch, she did the line work, and then I went in and finished the colouring and shading! The character is Honeydew, a dragon oc that belongs to Mossi. I like how it turned out. Art swaps are a lot of fun!

Autumn Forest

One of my favourite arts of all time, because I love the combination of red and blue. Red comes forward, and blue recedes, as cool colours do. This art always had a bit of a 3D effect to it that I love. Plus, the character is very cool, and the art is a commission for a friend of mine- KonnyArt on Instagram.

Overcast

An OC I was given by a random person on EA. His name is Overcast and he is a pegasus that lost his eye sight in an accident. He re-learned how to fly by sensing the change in air currents, and he practices at night in the mountains to avoid running into any other pegasi.

The Glade

This was one of my most difficult commissions, since I'm not all that comfortable with drawing detailed backgrounds. However, out of all the backgrounds I could try drawing, forests are my 'specialty'. I enjoy painting tree trunks, but the leaves are the hard part.

This drawing is a commission for bepis on Discord.

Isa

This cute OC belongs to Isabae on EA! I was trying a new quick and sketchy style, and I really like how this turned out. I learned about how to create the illusion of depth by drawing a simple foreground, midground, and background.

Endless

This art is my original horror character, Noah Harren, otherwise known as the 'Black Cat'. He was born with a curse that allows him to see into someone's future against his will, however, he can only see traumatic events in that person's future. Most of the time, these are horrible accidents and death.

He wears bandages around his eyes, because as long as he can't see someone, he won't see any future visions. Regardless, it seems that poor Noah causes misfortune wherever he goes.
