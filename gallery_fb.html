Gallery - Fullbody Art

Normal Eeveelution

A fun commission for Twiconda on Instagram! They asked me to draw their concept for a normal-type eeveelution. I like the fluffiness! It was fun to draw something that wasn't a pony, so I hope I can draw more pokemon.

Nevermore

An older art for Nevermore on CAtm of their OC. I love drawing glow effects so this was fun! My human style needs more developing, but I really like how this one turned out, especially considering how fast I was able to produce it. Success!

Golden

I designed this OC for Minetane on EA and they asked me to make a reference sheet of them! It was my biggest commission yet at the time, so I really went all out. It turned out so great! It is one of my proudest arts, especially considering how much the person loves the oc I designed for them.

Serein

I love designing characters. Adoptables, if you will. This particular adoptable is one of my favourite designs-- based on the word 'serein'. I referenced a twilight sky. She was quickly adopted by someone and I hope they enjoy her design as much as I do!

Soso Davis

Another prize art for the lovely Soso Davis on EA. This character has a lot of hair- a LOT of it. So, I drew them sleeping in their hair. I experimented with my line weight in this one and spent a much longer period of time refining the line art. It was definitely worth it, in my opinion! The colours purple and yellow work really well together here and make this art really pop!

Nihongo

A simple yet striking colour palette and character. I really enjoy working with limited palettes.

Cheza

This art is fan art for my favourite nuzlocke comic character, Cheza, from Chiakiro's amazing nuzlocke on Deviant. It's still ongoing and, if you're a fan of pokemon, it's definitely worth the read! It's a very dark, heart-felt comic, and I have been reading it since early high school. It's nearly complete now, only a few strips away from completion.
